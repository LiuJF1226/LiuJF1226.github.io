Jinfeng Liu

I am currently a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Dan Xu, at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I received my Bachelor's Degree and Master's Degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2021 and 2024, advised by Prof. Jie Yang and Prof. Wei Liu.

My primary research interests lie in 3D vision. My past research experience includes vision-language pretraining, depth estimation, and 3D/4D Gaussian Splatting. Currently, I am particularly interested in 3D/4D reconstruction, spatial intelligence, and world models. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in collaborating on these topics.
